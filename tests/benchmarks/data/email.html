Invitation to Speak at the 2026 European Conference on Applied AI Systems

Berlin, Germany · September 14–16, 2026

Dear Dr. Elena Weiss,

On behalf of the organizing committee of the 2026 European Conference on Applied AI Systems, I am pleased to invite you to give an invited talk during this year’s conference program.

Your recent work on reliable multi-agent coordination and evaluation frameworks for autonomous systems has been of great interest to our program committee. We believe your perspective would be highly valuable to our attendees, particularly researchers and practitioners working at the intersection of AI safety, software engineering, and human-centered automation.
